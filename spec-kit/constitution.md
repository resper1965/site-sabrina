# Constitution: Sabrina Barros Blog

## Core Mission

To establish a premier digital presence for Sabrina Barros, bridging the gap between clinical dentistry and high-level health insurance executive insights. The blog serves as a platform for expertise in health, supplementary health, and wellness.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Professional Elegance**: The design must reflect the dual nature of the personaâ€”clinical precision and executive leadership.
2. **Modern & Light**: Use a clean aesthetic with ample whitespace, refined typography, and a "light" feel (not heavy or cluttered).
3. **Information Integrity**: Health and insurance information must be presented clearly and authoritatively.
4. **User-Centric Design**: Prioritize ease of reading and navigation across all devices.
5. **Brand Consistency**: Maintain a cohesive visual identity that stays "elegant" and "modern."

## Technical Standards

- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for flexibility and performance.
- **UI Components**: Shadcn UI for accessible, beautiful primitives.
- **Content**: MDX/Contentlayer for manageable blog posts.
- **Performance**: High Lighthouse scores, optimized images.
