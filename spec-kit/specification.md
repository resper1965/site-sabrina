# Product Specification: Sabrina Barros Blog

## User Persona

**Sabrina Barros**: A modern professional with a dual career path.

- **Clinical Side**: Dentist with a focus on comprehensive care and wellness.
- **Executive Side**: Leader in the supplementary health (private insurance) sector.
- **Voice**: Professional, insightful, approachable, and authoritative.

## Audience

- Healthcare professionals and executives.
- Patients looking for wellness tips.
- Individuals interested in the dynamics of the private health insurance market in Brazil.

## Features

### 1. Visual Identity

- **Modern & Elegant**: Minimalism with a touch of luxury.
- **Palette**: Soft creams, deep blues or greens (professional yet calming), and high-contrast typography.
- **Logo**: Typographic focused logo (Sabrina Barros | Saúde & Gestão).

### 2. Homepage (Landing)

- **Hero Section**: Personal introduction that highlights the dual expertise.
- **Featured Articles**: Highlights of recent or crucial posts.
- **Quick Links**: Categories like "Health", "Management", "Tips".

### 3. Blog Module

- **Listing**: Clean cards with clear categories and read times.
- **Search & Filter**: Ability to filter by category.
- **Reading Experience**: Typography prioritized for long-form reading. Large headings, readable font sizes, and optimized line height.

### 4. About Sabrina

- A dedicated section or page detailing her journey from the clinic to executive leadership.

### 5. Contact/Newsletter

- Subtle call to action for networking or subscription.

## Success Metrics

- **Visual Impact**: Cohesive design that feels "vibrant" and "premium."
- **Readability**: High contrast and clean layout.
- **Responsiveness**: Flawless experience on mobile and desktop.
